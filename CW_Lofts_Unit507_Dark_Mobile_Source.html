Section 01
Property Overview
Great

CW Lofts is a genuine historic conversion — originally the 1927 Cream of Wheat headquarters and factory, redesigned by Walter H. Wheeler and clad in buff brick with Mankato stone. After Kraft Foods closed the Minneapolis operations, the building was redeveloped in 2007 into approximately 118 loft condos. Unit 507 sits on the 5th floor, which at CW Lofts includes select penthouse-tier units that sit above the roofline of the main building.
